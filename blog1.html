Summer vacation is a funny thing. Days before it begins, every student’s mind echoes the same thought: “Oh, I can’t wait for this to start!” But, true to its tricky nature, by the time it ends, most of us are left wondering, “How did an entire month vanish in the blink of an eye?” This rather confusing tomfoolery is what I'm going to document—and in doing so, bring to the surface my thoughts and misadventures.
